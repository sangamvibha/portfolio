Holi Special

The task was to produce a reel for Holi on behalf of an online coaching class, aimed at promoting their discount offer while ensuring the content resonated with their target audience: students in classes 11-12th. Collaborating with my colleague, Sharanya Ramesh, we conceptualized the idea and crafted the script. Subsequently, I utilized Procreate Dreams to animate the reel, followed by incorporating sound effects and recording my voice for the background voice-over.
