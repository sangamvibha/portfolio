Block Printing

DOCUMENTARY

This documentary delves into the intricate art of Block Printing, covering the entire process from block carving to fabric printing. Collaborating with my college mates Radha Somani and Sana Chopade, we meticulously captured each step of the process through our own lens. Upon gathering all the footage, we embarked on planning the video, during which I improved my editing skills to meet the project's requirements. This included delving into sound editing, which proved to be an intriguing learning experience. Additionally, I also explored color editing to ensure the visual narrative of the documentary was engaging. Through this project, not only did we showcase the beauty of Block Printing, but we also developed valuable skills in videography and post-production editing, enhancing our abilities as storytellers in the visual medium.
