The Sambhar Tragedy

As part of our Graphic Narrative module assignment, I created a single-panel comic depicting a childhood memory. In this particular moment, I vividly recall walking with my plate when a sudden tragedy struck: my mother accidentally spilled sambhar on my stomach. Hence, I titled it "The Sambhar Tragedy," a fitting name for the incident. The comic aims to capture the intense emotions of the moment: the shock and tension evident on my mother's face, juxtaposed with the fear reflected in my eyes. It's a snapshot of a childhood mishap that left a lasting impression, told through the medium of graphic storytelling.
